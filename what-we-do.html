What We Do

Major Lens helps you choose the right major, and understand it before you start.

Major Lens is a guidance platform designed for students who are unsure about their major or don’t fully understand what they chose. It combines clear explanations, real expectations, and structured guidance to help students make better decisions before university begins.

Step 1

Start with your situation

Students start by identifying what they need — either choosing a major or understanding the one they already picked.

Step 2

Get clear, simple explanations

Majors are explained in simple terms, so students can quickly understand what each option actually involves.

Step 3

Get the right guidance

Students are guided toward the right type of support based on their situation, helping them take the next step with more clarity.

Step 4

Understand what to expect

Students learn how demanding a major really is, including the workload, difficulty, and type of learning involved.

Service features

What the service actually provides.

Major choice guidance

Students who are unsure can explore different majors based on their interests, strengths, and way of learning.

Major reality preview

Students can see what their chosen major is actually like, including workload, difficulty, and first-year expectations.

Guided support

Students are directed toward the right type of guidance based on their situation, helping them take the next step with confidence.

How it solves the problem

It doesn’t replace advising. It makes it easier to access and understand.

Instead of expecting students to figure everything out on their own, the service organizes the questions students usually have and guides them toward the right information and support.

What students gain

  • Know whether they need to choose a major or better understand one
  • See what their major will actually be like before starting
  • Get clear next steps based on their situation
  • Feel more confident about their decision
