About Major Lens

A student-focused advising service built on real research.

Major Lens was created to turn research on student expectations, workload challenges, and major mismatch into a practical, easy-to-use digital service. It is designed to help students choose more confidently and understand what to expect before university begins.

Why it was founded

Because students need clearer expectations before university starts.

Research shows that students often enter university with strong expectations,then discover that the academic experience is different and the workload is more demanding than they expected. Major Lens is designed to address that gap.

What it is

A guidance-first student support service.

Major Lens is designed as a guidance platform that helps students make better-informed decisions about their major. It focuses on providing clear information, realistic expectations, and early access to the support students need before they begin university.

Values

Three ideas shape everything Major Lens does.

1

Clarity before commitment

Students should understand a major before they commit their time, energy, and expectations to it.

2

Guidance before confusion

Instead of waiting until students feel overwhelmed, Major Lens helps them ask the right questions earlier.

3

Research before assumptions

Major Lens is grounded in real student research and survey findings, not guesswork or generic advice.

Helping students choose a major, or understand the one they already chose, with more clarity and earlier guidance.
